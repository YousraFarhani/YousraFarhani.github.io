Research

Quantum Compilation & Error Correction

Niels Bohr Institute · NQCP · Nov 2025 – present

I build the software infrastructure for fault-tolerant quantum computers — optimizing how quantum circuits are compiled, corrected, and distributed across hardware nodes so that quantum chemistry and beyond becomes computationally feasible.
